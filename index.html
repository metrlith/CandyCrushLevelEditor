To use blockers, use the pink sticky note at the end of the blockers section for every static blocker. This does not include Licorice Swirls and Locks.

To use Candy Cannons you need to know that the Maximum means the maximum amount of items there can be on the board. The Interval means that if you reached the maximum, and let's say the interval is two, the game will spawn another single unit of that item after two moves.
To use teleporters, place a pink teleporter note on the exit teleporter and a blue one on the entry teleporter.
